Support

We'd love to hear from you: your ideas, your experiences, your problems with Aquamacs.


Documentation is readily available - please look there before contacting us.

  • Manual in Help menu,
  • Aquamacs Wiki - You may also use the Wiki to add and tips for customizing and improving Aquamacs.
  • Google: all our mailing lists are indexed.


Bug reports

  • Report bugs with the "Send Bug Report" function (Help menu) from within Aquamacs.
  • Please respect our Bug Reporting Guidelines.
  • If you are unable to use the bug reporting function, please send your bug report to the Aquamacs bug reports address by directly e-mailing aquamacs-bugs@aquamacs.org (no subscription required).
  • Archives on Google Groups

Questions should be directed to the Emacs-on-OS X mailing list.

  • Subscribe here.
  • Archives on GMANE
