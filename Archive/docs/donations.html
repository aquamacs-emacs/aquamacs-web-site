Support the Aquamacs Project

Because of a change in the maintainers, donations ended as of 30 September 2019, and will resume with the upcoming release of Aquamacs 3.6. Details will be available here at that time.

If you like Aquamacs, consider giving money (below) or your time.

Aquamacs is free software, but producing it costs a lot of time and also some real cash. From the time the Aquamacs project began in 2004 until 2019, a single developer has produced and combined the majority of the code that makes Aquamacs unique compared to a standard Emacs implementation. In 2019, a new maintainer took over the majority of the continuing maintenance work. Several other volunteers have helped by reporting bugs, making helpful suggestions, adding documentation, helping with support, and contributing special packages.

Your donation is an unrestricted gift - we do not promise anything in return. However, we are grateful for your support.

Supporters may occasionally receive emails with special updates about the project.

Corporate Sponsorships

Refund Policy

Gifts to the Aquamacs project are non-refundable.

Privacy Policy

The Aquamacs project will not pass on your personal details to third parties, nor will it allow the use of your personal details for marketing on behalf of third parties. The project may store your personal details to facilitate communications with its supporters through e-mail.
